Our Heritage

A Warm Welcome
in the Heart of Potos

Hotel Olympion is centrally located in the charming Potos village of Thassos, just 70 meters from the nearest beach. We offer traditional Greek hospitality and modern comforts for a pleasant stay on Thassos island.

Our air-conditioned rooms have laminate floors, simple furnishings, and a private balcony with garden or area views. Start your day with our buffet breakfast served on our covered outdoor terrace.

  • ◆ Just 70 meters from Potos Beach
  • ◆ Daily buffet breakfast on outdoor terrace
  • ◆ 24-hour front desk & free Wi-Fi in public areas
